Tabio

Effortless tab management.

Tabio is a simple chrome extension designed to make dealing with lots of tabs a whole lot easier.

Install Chrome Extension

Forget about billions of tiny tabs.

Just open the extension and scroll through a vertical list of all your open tabs.

Get what you want faster.

The quick search feature allows you to easily find the tab you're looking for.

Speedy navigation.

Use your arrow keys to quickly scroll through your tabs without ever leaving the keyboard.
