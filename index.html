Fuzzy Search

The fuzzy search feature allows you to quickly and easily find the tab you're looking for.

Keyboard Accessible

Speed up your workflow using Tabio's serveral time-saving keyboard shortcuts. Here's the list.

Drag & Drop

Tabio makes it easier than ever to organize your tabs. Simply drag and drop to reorder.

Multiple Windows

Tabio compiles a list of all open tabs across all browser windows, making it even easier to find the tab you're looking for.

Omnibox Support

Tabio will soon leverage one of Chrome's best features, allowing you to manage tabs directly from the omnibox.
